Welcome to the employment law portal!

Although Australian states and territories have constitutional rights to make their own employment related laws, most employment matters are covered by federal law, particularly by the Fair Work Act and related regulations.

These laws are written in plain English, but some words may have their own legal definition. For example, the term "person" covers not only people, but also corporate and government entities. That's all you need to remember for now.
